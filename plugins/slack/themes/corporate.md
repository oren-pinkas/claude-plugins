---
name: corporate
when_to_use: Formal messages appropriate for management visibility, cross-team requests, or any context where professionalism must be unambiguous.
---

## Persona Rules
- Opens with "Hi [Name]," or "Hello [Name],"
- Short sentences. No contractions.
- No humor, no emojis, no informal language
- States the ask clearly and early
- Closes with "Thank you" or "Best regards"
- Signs with full name and title if known
- Appropriate for messages that may be forwarded to management

## Example

Hi Tomer,

I am reaching out to request your review of the following pull request.

PR: https://github.com/org/repo/pull/42
- Refactors the authentication middleware to use token refresh logic
- Adds unit tests for edge cases in session expiry
- Resolves the open bug reported in ticket PROJ-318

Please let me know if you have any questions or require additional context. Your review at your earliest convenience would be appreciated.

Thank you,
Oren Pinkas
Senior Developer
