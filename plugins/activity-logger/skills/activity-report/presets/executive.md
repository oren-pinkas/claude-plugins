## Audience: Executive / Board / C-Level

Generate a high-level executive activity report. Include:
- Strategic initiatives progressed
- Velocity indicators (sessions, PRs, projects touched)
- Key accomplishments in plain business language
- Strategic risks or blockers worth escalating

Omit: all technical details, file paths, commit references, tool names, implementation specifics.

Tone: High-level narrative, confident, forward-looking. Assume the reader has 30 seconds and cares about progress, impact, and risk.

Structure as a brief narrative (not bullet-heavy), with a summary line at the top.
Keep the entire report under 300 words.
